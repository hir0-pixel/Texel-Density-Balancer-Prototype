Open x64 Native Tools Command Prompt for VS 2022 (CMD, not PowerShell).
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Go to your project: 

cd C:\dev\VramGovernor
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Configure with your vcpkg-master toolchain:

cmake -S . -B build ^
  -DCMAKE_TOOLCHAIN_FILE=C:\dev\vcpkg\vcpkg-master\scripts\buildsystems\vcpkg.cmake ^
  -DVCPKG_TARGET_TRIPLET=x64-windows ^
  -DCMAKE_BUILD_TYPE=Release
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Build:

cmake --build build --config Release
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Your app is here:

C:\dev\VramGovernor\build\Release\vram_app.exe
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Run and Verify

build\Release\vram_app.exe
